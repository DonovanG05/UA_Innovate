About the Grading System

Each area is scored based on:

  • Raw Score: Total MTCO₂e from trucking, factory, energy, refrigeration & packaging
  • Initiative Deduction: Points removed for RVM deployments, EV fleet %, and renewable energy %
  • Final Score: Raw − Deduction (lower = better)
